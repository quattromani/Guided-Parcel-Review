Tax sensitivity draft

The model studies each property one year at a time. Instead of asking whether taxes follow value directly, it measures how much ETR changed after levy/rate and credit effects were absorbed.

1

Normalize tax pressure

ETR tells us how much tax each dollar of value carried in that year.

ETR_y = Tax_y ÷ Value_y
2

Measure yearly movement

Each parcel contributes a sequence of paired changes, such as 2024 → 2025.

ΔTax = (Tax_2 ÷ Tax_1) − 1
3

Project with ETR response

The median ETR response is the historical offset to the value change.

Tax_hat = (1 + ΔV) × (1 + med ΔETR) − 1
