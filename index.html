Equalization Context

Is the value base being checked for fairness?

Equalization reads from the property record into the valuation group or market area, then into the class and countywide study. It does not stop market values from moving or set the tax levy. It asks whether properties are being assessed at the required level and with reasonable uniformity, so the tax base is not tilted before levies are applied.

Decision check

What can equalization tell you?

Equalization can explain whether the assessment system is generally level and uniform. It does not prove an individual parcel outcome by itself, and it does not control tax levies.
